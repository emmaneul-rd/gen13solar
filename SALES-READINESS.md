## Embudo
**FUNNEL_PRESENT**

The home page contains all seven funnel steps:

1. Problema claro — framed via "Lower bills. More control." and the closing band "Stop renting all your electricity. Start building energy independence."
2. Promesa clara — "Energy independence starts here." / "Solar, simplified." with a benefit-led hero lead.
3. Autoridad/confianza — trustbar (DFW local, licensed installers, premium equipment, financing, residential & commercial), labeled planning metrics, "Why Gen 13 Solar" checklist, project gallery, three testimonials, FAQ.
4. Oferta clara — six service cards, the solar savings estimator, and a five-step "how it works" process.
5. Reducción de riesgo — calculator disclaimer ("Educational estimate only… a site assessment… is required before any proposal"), FAQ (battery, cloudy days, property value, sizing), "Free bill review" step, "no obligation" calculator copy.
6. CTA repetido — hero (x2), calculator, closing CTA band (x2), persistent floating quote/call buttons, footer.
7. Cierre — contact form (FormSubmit.co), phone, email and floating actions.

## Psicología de ventas
- Claridad: HIGH — headline, lead, trustbar and metrics appear in the first screen; the value proposition is obvious in under 10 seconds.
- Deseo: HIGH — benefit-led language ("Lower bills. More control.") and a strong pain-to-promise close ("Stop renting all your electricity").
- Confianza: HIGH — authority signals (local DFW, licensed, 288+ client-reported projects, 10+ years) appear before any data is requested.
- Reducción de riesgo: GOOD — disclaimers, FAQ and "free / no-obligation" language lower uncertainty without inventing guarantees.
- CTA: GOOD — visible, repeated and non-aggressive; persistent floating actions keep contact one tap away.
- Conversión mobile: GOOD — CTA above the fold, no horizontal overflow, hamburger and floating buttons functional.
