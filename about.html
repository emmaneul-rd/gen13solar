Who we are

About Jorge Feliz

Jorge Feliz founded Gen 13 Solar with a clear mission: to help homeowners, businesses, churches and nonprofits take control of their energy costs through honest advice, quality workmanship and long-term value.

With a background in procurement, logistics and financial analysis, Jorge approaches each project from an investment perspective, not a sales perspective. Every system is carefully designed using professional-grade solar modeling tools so recommendations are backed by real data.

At Gen 13 Solar, trust is earned through transparency: clear communication, premium equipment, meticulous design and support that continues after installation.

Whether the goal is lowering electric bills, preparing for rising utility costs or investing in the future of a property, Jorge's focus is simple: give every client the knowledge and guidance needed to make a confident decision.
