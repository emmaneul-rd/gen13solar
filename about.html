Who we are

Local solar guidance grounded in real property needs.

At Gen 13 Solar, we are dedicated to powering a more sustainable future through practical solar and energy solutions.

Our work starts with listening. We evaluate electric usage, roof conditions, business priorities, financing preferences and long-term plans before recommending a system. The goal is not simply to place panels. It is to design an energy asset that fits the property and remains understandable after installation day.

Mission & vision

Clean energy should feel accessible, intelligent and useful.

Our mission

To make clean energy more accessible by helping build a distributed, intelligent solar and energy-storage network across the communities we serve.

Our vision

A future where energy management is cleaner, smarter and more effective, and where households and organizations have greater control over how their power is produced and used.

How we work

Four principles behind every recommendation.

Evidence first

Usage, shade, roof geometry and utility rules come before system size.

Clear commitments

Scope, assumptions, warranties and next steps should be visible and understandable.

Community respect

Advice should fit the customer, not force the customer into a template.

Long-term service

Monitoring, maintenance and support matter after the system is turned on.
